Monday Morning Huddle Reporting

Powers the Monday Morning Huddle with a dual-view report: last week's schedule-vs-actual review (variance, OT, absences, missed clock-outs, behavior patterns) and this week's projected hours and scheduled OT. Detects cross-location overtime that Analytics Hub misses. Emails two-tier summaries to directors and managers, and automatically archives every huddle presentation.

Google Apps Script Google Sheets Auto-Emails
This is a Google Apps Script application. It runs inside Google Sheets and cannot be opened directly in a browser. Follow the setup steps below to deploy it to your Google account.

Features

  • Last Week Review: schedule vs actual hours, variance, OT, attendance flags
  • This Week Plan: projected scheduled hours and OT to catch problems before they happen
  • Cross-location OT detection (combines CH + DBU hours per employee)
  • Missed clock-out detection (90+ min past scheduled end)
  • Name normalization + alias map for consistent matching across exports
  • Two-tier email reports: full detail for directors, summary for managers
  • Automatic Monday 5 PM archive of all huddle presentation tabs
  • Historical reports: worst offenders, OT trends, chronic lateness, individual timelines

Setup Steps

  1. Create a new Google Sheet for the Monday Huddle system.
  2. Open Extensions → Apps Script and paste each .gs file.
  3. Create an HTML file named Upload and paste Upload.html.
  4. Reload the sheet, then click Schedule Variance → Initialize Sheet to create all required tabs and populate Settings defaults.
  5. Open the Settings tab: fill in Director Email Recipients, Manager Email Recipients, and Trigger Alert Email.
  6. Click Schedule Variance → Setup Weekly Archive (Monday 5 PM) to enable automatic huddle archiving.
  7. Test with a known week: upload last week's schedule + punches and this week's schedules via Upload & Analyze.
