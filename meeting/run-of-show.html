2. The team-member experience

0:04 – 0:14
Gallery → Uniform Request demo  |  Gallery → Time-Off Request demo
  • Uniform Request: pick items and sizes, and the team member sees the price of each item and exactly what comes out of each paycheck before submitting. No surprises on payday.
  • Time-Off Request: a team member puts in their own PTO from their phone — pick dates, submit. No catching a manager, no waiting on a text. Both forms are bilingual (English & Spanish).
  • The point: dignity and time back. People handle their own stuff without having to ask permission for everything.
“This is the part that actually moves retention — people feel respected when they don’t have to chase a manager to take a day off or guess what a uniform will cost them.”

3. Where it lands — Payroll
